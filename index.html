Course description

Textbook and other supporting materials

The theoretical module will follow:

• Introduction to Quantitative Genetics (4th Edition) by Falconer & Mackay

• Breeding for quantitative traits in plants (3th Edition) by Bernardo.

• Introduction to genetic analysis by Griffiths (10th Edition) by Wessler, Carrol & Doebley.

Lectures notes, videos and R code will be made available through the workshop’s website.

Workshop objectives

In the theoretical section attendees will learn basic quantitative genetics concepts and basic statistical methods for genetic data analysis focus in genomic prediction.

Workshop Format

All the lectures will be prerecorded and a live session of 1 hours of discussion is going be hold for each 3 lectures. To increase the interaction in the discussion sessions the attendees are encouraged to send at least one question about the videos and/or papers that will be discuss in each session.
